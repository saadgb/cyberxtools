Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about CyberX Tools and our online utilities

General Questions

What is CyberX Tools?

CyberX Tools is a collection of free online utilities designed to help developers, testers, and digital professionals with common tasks. Our tools include address generation and age calculation utilities, with more features being added regularly.

Are these tools really free?

Yes, all our tools are completely free to use. There are no hidden charges or subscription fees. We maintain our service through minimal, non-intrusive advertising.

Do I need to create an account?

No, you don't need to create an account or register to use our tools. All features are available instantly without any registration requirement.

Address Generator FAQ

Are the generated addresses real?

No, all addresses generated are completely fictional and randomly created for testing purposes only. They should not be used for any real-world applications or fraud.

How many addresses can I generate at once?

You can generate up to 50 addresses in a single request. This limit helps ensure optimal performance for all users.

Are the postal codes and phone numbers format-accurate?

Yes, our generator follows country-specific formats for postal codes and phone numbers to provide realistic test data.

Age Calculator FAQ

How accurate is the age calculator?

Our age calculator provides precise calculations down to the day, taking into account leap years and varying month lengths.

Can I calculate future dates?

No, our age calculator currently only works with past and present dates. Future dates are not supported to maintain accuracy.

What date format should I use?

The calculator uses your browser's date picker, ensuring consistent date format handling across different regions.

Privacy & Security

Do you store any user data?

No, we don't store any user data or generated content. All operations are performed in real-time and nothing is saved on our servers.

Is my information secure?

Yes, all our tools run directly in your browser. We don't collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Do you use cookies?

We use only essential cookies necessary for the website's basic functionality and analytics to improve our services.

Technical Support

What browsers are supported?

Our tools work with all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. We recommend keeping your browser updated for the best experience.

What should I do if a tool isn't working?

First, try refreshing your browser and clearing your cache. If the issue persists, please contact us through our support channels.

How can I report a bug?

If you encounter any issues or bugs, please use our contact form to report them. Include as much detail as possible to help us investigate.
